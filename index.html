Who am I?

I am an experienced engineering manager with more than 16 years in Software Development. I have worked for two companies in two different industries over that period - OpenBet Technologies and Sky Plc.

At OpenBet I progressed through a range of roles, the highlights being starting as a Software Developer, then moving to Senior Developer and Technical Lead and finally to Development Manager, delivering front-end websites and back-end sportsbook trading, gaming and account management software for many of OpenBet’s biggest clients such as Coral, Betfair, Rank and Tote to mention a few. I managed teams split across different OpenBet sites - London, Athens and Romania. I also worked onsite at a new client’s Canadian office for the duration of a project, in New Brunswick, Canada, leading a team in the development and rollout of a new front-end sportsbook and back-end trading solution for Atlantic Lottery.
